Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions

Who qualifies for a free cane?

Any U.S. military veteran who needs a walking cane. There are no income requirements or service-era restrictions.

How long does it take to receive my cane?

Each cane is custom-built by hand. Typical build time is 2–4 weeks depending on complexity, plus shipping time.

What customization options are available?

You can choose wood type (mahogany, walnut, oak, and more), finish (Danish oil, tung oil, polyurethane), brass fittings, engravings, handle style, and height.

Is there really no cost to me?

None. Materials, labor, and shipping are all funded by donations and sponsors. You will never be charged.

How do I get help with VA benefits?

Fill out the same intake form and indicate you need VA benefits support. We’ll connect with you to walk through the process.
