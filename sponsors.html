Our Sponsors

The businesses and individuals who make free canes for veterans possible.

North Point Lumber

Sponsor since February 27, 2026

North Point Lumber is a full-service sawmill, lumber yard, and woodshop servicing North Texas since 2020. Their local exotics are shipped across the globe.

Baker Hardwoods

Sponsor

Baker Hardwoods is a family-owned and operated hardwood lumber business dedicated to providing the highest quality materials and service in North Texas. They stock over 40 species of domestic and exotic hardwoods and operate a full-service sawmill, dry kiln, and mill shop. A commitment to doing things right.

Veritiv

Sponsor

Veritiv, a North American business-to-business packaging and supply chain solutions company, centers its organizational culture around the CODE of Values: Cultivate Innovation, Own It, Deliver and Delight, and Embrace the Green.

Visit website

Catalem LLC

Sponsor

Catalem LLC is the maker of Culminari for Veterans, a free app built by a service-disabled veteran to help fellow veterans see the bigger picture of their benefits in one place. It projects VA compensation, Social Security retirement benefits, Medicare costs, and survivor benefits forward over time — with interactive tools to explore options and flag milestones along the way. No advice, no guarantees, just clear math using published government rates.
